Our Mission

Welcome to our site! The mission of Smart Ride is to provide sustainable routes for users who are looking to get from point a to point b while being a little kinder to the planet! Wherever you need to go, our app will provide you possible routes while giving you the choice to pick options that are more sustainable like biking, walking, or using a scooter! Just login and then select 'New Trip' above to get started!


Once you've chosen a route, the information from that route is saved to your dashboard so that you can easily track your weekly stats and progress, view which modes of transportation you use most often, and compare your weekly carbon emissions versus the national average.


Studies show that having friends with like minded goals help you achieve them, so we incorporated messaging and community features so that you can challenge your friends, organize carpools, and share tips and resources! Click 'Login' above (if you're not logged in already) and check out your own page to make a post!
